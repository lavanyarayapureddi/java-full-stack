Sri Vasavi Institute of Engineering and Technology (SVIET), located in Nandamuru, Andhra Pradesh, is an engineering college established in 2007 by industrialists. It's situated in a serene, rural environment away from the city, offering a pollution-free learning atmosphere. SVIET aims to be a leading engineering institution in rural India, focusing on providing a creative learning environment, promoting industry-institute interaction, fostering good citizens, and improving technical excellence
